How Tasks Work

1. Choose a Task

Select a task from the available options that interests you

2. Go to Task

Click the "Go to Task" button to visit the third-party site

3. Complete Requirements

Follow all the instructions carefully to fulfill the requirements

4. Get Paid

Mark as completed and receive your reward instantly

Task Completion Tips

  • Read requirements carefully

    Make sure you understand all the steps before starting

  • Complete tasks fully

    Don't skip steps or your completion may be rejected later

  • Prioritize high-paying tasks

    Focus on tasks with better rewards to maximize earnings

  • Level up your account

    Higher levels can access more tasks per day

  • Enable notifications

    Get alerted when high-paying tasks become available

  • Check back daily

    New tasks are added regularly throughout the day
