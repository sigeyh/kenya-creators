Frequently Asked Questions

How much commission do I earn?

You earn 10% of everything your referrals earn from watching videos and completing tasks.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

No, there's no limit. You can refer as many people as you want and earn commission from all of them.

How long do I earn commission for?

You earn commission for the lifetime of your referral's account. There's no time limit.

When do I receive my referral earnings?

Referral earnings are added to your balance instantly whenever your referrals earn money.

Can I refer myself using another account?

No, self-referrals are not allowed and may result in account suspension.
